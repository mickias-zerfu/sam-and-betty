Meet the Groom

Samuel Heiru

I want to express myself as someone who was once a sinner, living in darkness. But through the love of our Holy Father, revealed in His Son, Jesus Christ, I have come into the light from darkness, into blessed hope from hopelessness, into deep joy from depression, and into strength from brokenness. Now, I live by faith, sanctified by the Holy Spirit and guided by the Scriptures.

Bethelhem S.

When I think about myself, the first thing that sparks in my mind and heart at the same time is the depth of Christ's love for us—even when we were against Him. As Paul says in Romans 5:10, "For if, while we were God’s enemies, we were reconciled to Him through the death of His Son, how much more, having been reconciled, shall we be saved through His life!"
I cannot even imagine what our fate would be without Him. As John 10:16 says, "I have other sheep that are not of this sheep pen. I must bring them also. They too will listen to my voice, and there shall be one flock and one shepherd." I am one of those sheep—called into His fold and safely under His care as my Shepherd.
